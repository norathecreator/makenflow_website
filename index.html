    About Makenflôw

    We are the brand inspired by our travels to Bali, an Indonesian archipelago found in Sunda Islands, and the sustainable village boasting a multitude of landscapes and experiences.

    The villagers passion to live as organically as possible and preserve the beauty of Bali evoked the start of our own journey to a more sustainable lifestyle-thus Makenflôw was born.

    We hope to bring beauty to your space without disrupting the natural beauty of the world.
